Laser Animations.

Rotations work clockwise, starting with Down (1)



Idle sequence: play 5 frames forward, then 5 frames reverse.

Run: play 10 frames forward loop.

Ready Gun: play 5 frames forward to bring weapon into position. Play 5 frames in reverse after Shoot animation, to lower gun back to idle position.

Shoot: Play 10 frames forward.

Death: Play 10 frames forward... hold on final frame.(maybe try alternating last 2 frames continuously).
